THE HONOURABLE MRS MERVYN TALBOYS: (Unbuttoning her gauntlet violently.) I'll do no such thing. Pigdog and always was ever since he was pupped! To dare address me! I'll flog him black and blue in the public streets. I'll dig my spurs in him up to the rowel.

BLOOM: (Trembling, beginning to obey.) The weather has been so warm.
